About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at the University at Buffalo, advised by Dr. Jun Xia. My research focuses on photoacoustic imaging, deep learning, image denoising, and intelligent vascular diagnostics. I specialize in developing advanced medical imaging technologies for chronic wound assessment and vascular health monitoring.
