About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at the University at Buffalo, advised by Dr. Jun Xia. My research focuses on photoacoustic imaging, deep learning, image denoising, and intelligent diagnostic tools for chronic vascular diseases and wound healing.
