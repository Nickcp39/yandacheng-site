About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University at Buffalo (UB), advised by Prof. Ifeoma Nwogu. My research focuses on computer vision, large language models, 3D human modeling, and AI-generated content with applications in sign language, education, and VR/AR. I hold a Master's from Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) where I worked under Prof. Xinyu Yang.
